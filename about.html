About Us

We are a group of students from the URJC who have jumped into developing games for the enjoyment of as many people as possible.

We specialize in video games of the genre "PARTY GAME", ideal for the whole family or a group of friends who want to try new experiences and have fun together.

Right now we have two projects, IMPROVINCI and KiWin (soon), which you can take a look at on this website.
