About Us

We are a group of students from the King Juan Carlos University who are developing games, with a focus on online mutiplayer.

Our first game, Improvinci, is a party game that's perfect for playing with your family or your friends.

Our second game, Eldritchess, is a competitive strategy game with familiar pieces and exciting strategies.
